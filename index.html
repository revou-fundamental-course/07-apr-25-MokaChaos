Our Profile

Hi! I'm MokaChaos, an independent digital illustrator specializing in anime and manga-style artwork. With a background in traditional drawing and a love for Japanese pop culture, I bring characters to life through expressive lines, dynamic compositions, and bold color palettes. I focus on character design, storytelling through visuals, and creating art that connects with the anime community. I work with both individual clients and creative teams, offering commissions, prints, and collaborative projects.
